# Bundled fonts

All fonts here are under the SIL Open Font License (OFL) and are bundled with
the app (loaded at runtime by `ui.c`).

| File | Font | Source | License |
|---|---|---|---|
| `dotmatrix.ttf` | Dot Matrix (Regular) | [DanielHartUK/Dot-Matrix-Typeface](https://github.com/DanielHartUK/Dot-Matrix-Typeface) | OFL |
| `seg.ttf` | DSEG7 Classic (Bold) — 7-segment | [keshikan/DSEG](https://github.com/keshikan/DSEG) | OFL |
| `label.ttf` | Archivo (Medium/Regular) | [Omnibus-Type/Archivo](https://github.com/Omnibus-Type/Archivo) | OFL |
| `mono.ttf` | Space Mono (Regular) | [googlefonts/spacemono](https://github.com/googlefonts/spacemono) | OFL |

These replace the proprietary Nothing (Ndot/NType82) and TE (Univers) faces with
free, redistributable equivalents that capture the same aesthetic. Full OFL
license texts are available at each source repository.
